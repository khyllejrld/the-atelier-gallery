Who We Are

Our Story

The Atelier Gallery is a curated digital archive built as an academic web design showcase, bringing together two painters whose work reshaped how we look at the world: Monet's shifting Impressionist light and Van Gogh's expressive Post-Impressionist color. The collection stays deliberately small, favoring a close look at a few defining works over a comprehensive archive. It's maintained by a small team, and we're always happy to hear from researchers, collectors, and anyone curious about the works on view.
