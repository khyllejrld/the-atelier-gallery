About the Project

The Atelier Gallery brings together two painters whose work reshaped how we look at the world: Monet's shifting Impressionist light and Van Gogh's expressive Post-Impressionist color. We keep the collection deliberately small, favoring a close look at a few defining works over a sprawling archive. We're a small team, and we're always happy to hear from collectors, researchers, and anyone curious about the works on view.
